About us

Our goal: to establish a leading enterprise in the field of artificial intelligence based on Dalian and radiating three provinces in Northeast China.

We focus on applying artificial intelligence and automation control related technologies to solve potential social problems and promote social progress and development.

  • Application development: WEB, APP, Salesforce, UiPath

  • Internet of Things: digital city, smart building, regulation of traffic and people flow, disaster prevention monitoring

  • Big data: behavioral data (consumption, education, social), geographic data statistics and forecasts (price, sightseeing)

  • Artificial intelligence: knowledge map, robotic driverless and path planning

  • Operation and maintenance: digital marketing, operation services, monitoring and maintenance

what are we going to do?

Smart bridgeTechnology is focused on the application of "5G, IoT, AI" technology, to develop and implement forward-looking solutions to solve problems in social development.
